CAPTAIN AMERICA

 

Captain America is the leader of the Avengers, and I first watched him in the movie Captain America: The Winter Soldier.

 

Although Captain America does not have a cool visual appearance, he has insane superhuman abilities.

 

 

Favourite Moment

My favourite moment of Captain America is when he uses the Mjolnir, which is Thor’s first hammer, to fight Thanos in Avengers: End Game.
