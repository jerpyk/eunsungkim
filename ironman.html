IRON MAN

 

The first Marvel movie that I watched was the first Iron Man. Watching Tony Stark fly around in his armor looked amazingly cool.

 

Tony Stark doesn’t have just one armor; he has a set displayed in his house! Iron Man’s suit gets advanced as he appears in more movies.

 

 

Favourite Moment

My favourite moment of Iron Man is the moment before he dies, which is when he snapped his finger with the Infinity Gauntlet in Avengers: End Game (sorry if I spoiled it).
