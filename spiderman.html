SPIDER-MAN

 

Since I was a kid, Spider-Man has been my favourite superhero. When I watched Spider-Man, I always wanted to fly through buildings of the city with the webs.

 

My first time seeing Spider-Man in movies was Sony's first Spider-Man. With Iron Man’s suit, Spider Man’s suit also gets advanced as more of his movies come out. Iron Man’s technology helps other heroes too!

 

 

Favourite Moment

My favourite moment of Spider-Man is when the three different Spider-Man (Sony’s Spider-Man, the Amazing Spider-Man, and the Marvel Cinematic Universe Spider-Man) fight against the villains of the three universes in Spider-Man: No Way Home.
